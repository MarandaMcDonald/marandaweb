Fancy wraping

Crafts

Newbaums bar tape, crafts

A high effort, low reward bike accent

It takes quite awhile to wrap this way, and I've only had it last awhile by using Newbaums cloth bar tape which has almost no padding. Its worked once for me to have a layer of the old gel bar tape underneath and braid over that, but eventually wore in a way to come apart after 2yrs of use. This is definitely a form over function choice.

Bullhorn bars done with white, yellow, black braids. Integrated brake cable, finished with twine

This set was done braiding 4 seperate runs, 2 white and the black and yellow. Took ages to start it right, and eventually with the curve involved the diamonds got uneven, but it really popped for awhile! I also applied a layer of shellac hoping it would be able to wipe clean but that did not really work, and made it extra stiff. As it got dirty it became quite a bit more subdued. Used the remaining bits for a polo mallet, about a year later here to compare color.
