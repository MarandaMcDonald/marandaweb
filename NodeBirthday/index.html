Node turned 15yrs old!

And I had the perfect way to make and chop the cake! I recently cobbled together a simple guillotine with aluminum rails salvaged from whatever Race Pace Columbia was tossing out as they were bought by Trek. Nice long rails with two 1/8in grooves so I welded a big piece of metal that was sitting around onto other bits that run in the groove, then the rails screw in to scrap wood alongside random thick stuff to raise it off the ground enough when the blade drops. A well functioning guillotine would stay perfectly parallel the whole length, this is not that- but it can chop a cake and melon! would love to try vs a whole hog roast someday.
