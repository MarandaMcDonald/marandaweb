Tall Bike Jousting!

Burning Van 2023, Burning Van 2024, Bike Kill 2024, Freak-For-All 2025

It looks more dangerous than it is

I think first time jousting was at Burning Van 2023 when I was first hooked on freakbikes and finding the communities. It looked pretty gnarly, but I was on active duty then so free healthcare, enough booze in the system and why not give it a go-

"Early in my "career" vs Richmond Dead Fucking Last's Sera"

How to

Its pretty simple, you get a squire or two who hold the bike steady as you mount, hopefully someones corrdinating with the opponent to ensure both parties are ready at the same time, then your squires push you off to meet your fate. Sometimes squires suck and launch you into the crowd, you don't have too much time to pedal or fix trajectories

"Eye on the prize"

Tools of the trade

All it takes are two tall bikes, ideally of the same design, and two lances- typically made of ~10ft of PVC with something soft duct taped to the front

"Facing Chicago Rat Watch's Justin, an even match"
